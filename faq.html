Frequently asked questions

Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us.

How do I enroll in a course? +

Open any course page and click "Enroll now." Free courses give you instant access; paid courses walk you through a short checkout first. You'll need an account — sign up is free.

Do courses expire? +

No. Once you're enrolled, you keep access to that course's lessons, resources, and updates for as long as your account exists.

How do certificates work? +

Certificates are issued automatically once you mark every lesson in a course complete. You can view, print, or save it as a PDF from the Certificates page in your dashboard.

Can I get a refund? +

Reach out through the contact page within 14 days of purchase and we'll take care of it.

How do assignments get graded? +

You upload your work (PDF, ZIP, DOCX, images, or code files) from the Assignments page. A real instructor reviews it and leaves a score and written feedback — you'll get a notification when it's ready.

I want to teach a course — how do I become an instructor? +

Select "Instructor" when you sign up, or contact us if you already have a student account and want to switch.

Are live classes recorded? +

Most are. If a replay is available, you'll find it on the Live Classes page under "Past & replays" shortly after the session ends.
